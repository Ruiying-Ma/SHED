The paper reports experiments with different word embeddings to initialize a state-of-the-art Bi-LSTM-CRF network for event detection and classification in Italian (following the EVENTI evaluation). It achieves a new state-of-the-art by improving F1 by 1.3 points for detection and 6.5 points for classification using a single-step approach, and shows that embeddings have a major impact on performance.
