4.1 Experimental Settings
The hyper-parameters that were determined during the validation is presented in Table 2. The preprocessing of the data was conducted by lower-casing all the words in the documents and removing numbers. This results in a vocabulary size of | V | = 29044. Words not present in the training set are considered unknown during testing. Also, in order to have fixed-size contexts around the ambiguous words, the padding and truncating are applied to them whenever needed.
