3M’s FY2022 operating margin decline was driven chiefly by a 2.4 percentage-point raw material and logistics cost headwind, foreign exchange translation impacts (decreasing operating income by approximately $271 million), lost income and stranded costs from divestitures (including the Food Safety split‑off and Aearo deconsolidation), and special items such as net litigation costs and PFAS manufacturing exit costs, partially offset by organic growth/productivity (benefits from strong pricing, spending discipline and prior restructuring) which contributed roughly +1.0–1.3 percentage points (with a −0.3pp impact from declines in disposable respirator demand).
