4 Conclusion
In this paper, we summarized the system design of our BioASQ 5B submission for factoid and list questions. We use a neural architecture which is trained end-to-end on the QA task. This approach has not been applied to BioASQ questions in pre- vious challenges. Our results show that our ap- proach achieves state-of-the art results on factoid questions and competitive results on list questions.
