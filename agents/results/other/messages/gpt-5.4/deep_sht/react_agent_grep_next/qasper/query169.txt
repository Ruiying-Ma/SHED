5. FUSION
Our primary system is the linear fusion of all the above six subsys- tems by BOSARIS Toolkit on SRE19 dev and eval [10]. Before the fusion, each score is calibrated by PAV method ( pav calibrate scores) on our development database. It is evaluated by the primary metric provided by NIST SRE 2019.
