Compared to the other systems reported, OpenTapioca is not state of the art overall: its F1 scores are 0.482/0.399 on AIDA-CoNLL, 0.087/0.515 on Microposts 2016, 0.870/0.858 on ISTEX-1000, and 0.335/0.310 on RSS-500 (micro/macro). It outperforms the others on ISTEX-1000, but is worse than AIDA on AIDA-CoNLL and worse than AIDA and FREME NER on Microposts 2016.
