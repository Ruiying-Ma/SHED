Upon completion of the Redemption, the Company obtained a controlling interest of 100% in Hemlock Semiconductor LLC and 80. 5% interest in Hemlock Semiconductor Operations LLC and began consolidating HSG. Management recognized a pre-tax gain of $498 million on the Company's previously held equity investment in HSG as a result of the consolidation resulting from the Redemption.
