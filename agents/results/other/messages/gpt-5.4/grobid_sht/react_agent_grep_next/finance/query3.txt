3M’s operating margin decreased to 19.1% in 2022 from 20.8% in 2021, and on an adjusted basis decreased to 20.8% from 22.2%. The adjusted year-on-year decline was driven by a 2.4 percentage-point raw material impact and a 0.3 percentage-point negative impact from declines in disposable respirator demand, partly offset by a 1.0 percentage-point benefit from organic growth/productivity and other factors, including strong pricing, spending discipline, and 2021 restructuring actions despite supply chain, geopolitical, and China COVID-related manufacturing headwinds.
