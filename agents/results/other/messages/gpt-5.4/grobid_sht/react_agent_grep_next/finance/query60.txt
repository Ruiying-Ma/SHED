Note 22 -Segment and Revenue Information
Our primary profitability measurements to review a segment's operating results are Earnings/(loss) from operations and operating margins. Revenue on commercial aircraft contracts is recognized at the point in time when an aircraft is completed and accepted by the customer.BDS engages in the research, development, production and modification of the following products and related services: manned and unmanned military aircraft and weapons systems, surveillance and engagement, strategic defense and intelligence systems, satellite systems and space exploration. BDS revenue is generally recognized over the contract term (over time) as costs are incurred.
