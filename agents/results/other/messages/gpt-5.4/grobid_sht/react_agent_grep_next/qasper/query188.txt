A. Dataset
We used the IDN Tagged Corpus proposed in [12] . The corpus contains 10K sentences and 250K tokens that are tagged manually. Due to the small size, 3 we used 5-fold cross-validation to split the corpus into training, development, and test sets. We did not split multi-word expressions but treated them as if they are a single token. All 5 folds of the dataset are available publicly 4 to serve as a benchmark for future work.
