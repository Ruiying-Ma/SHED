Compared with the traditional tdnn in [4] , it has wider context and interleaving dense layers between each two tdnn layers. The architecture of our etdnn network is shown in table 5 . It is the same as the etdnn architecture in [3] , except that the context of layer 5 of our system is t-3:t+3 instead of t-3, t, t+3.  The x-vector is extracted from layer 12 prior to the ReLU non-linearity. For the loss, we use additive margin softmax with m = 0.15 instead of traditional softmax loss or angular softmax loss. Additive margin softmax is proposed in [5] and then used in speaker verification in our paper [2] . It is easier to train and generally performs better than angular softmax.
