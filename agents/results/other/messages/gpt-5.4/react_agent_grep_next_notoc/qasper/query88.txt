Abstract
We focus on named entity recognition (NER) for Chinese social media. With massive unlabeled text and quite lim- ited labelled corpus, we propose a semi- supervised learning model based on B- LSTM neural network. To take advan- tage of traditional methods in NER such as CRF, we combine transition probabil- ity with deep learning in our model.  To bridge the gap between label accuracy and F-score of NER, we construct a model which can be directly trained on F-score. When considering the instability of F- score driven method and meaningful in- formation provided by label accuracy, we propose an integrated method to train on both F-score and label accuracy. Our in- tegrated model yields substantial improve- ment over previous state-of-the-art result.
