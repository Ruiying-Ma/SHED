4.1 Evaluation Scheme

SICK and JOCI are created by Human Judged , referring that hypotheses and premises are automatically paired while labels are generated by humans ( Poliak et al. , 2018 ). In order to maxi- mumly mitigate the impacts of annotation artifacts during evaluations, we train and validate models respectively on SNLI and MultiNLI and test on both SICK and JOCI.
