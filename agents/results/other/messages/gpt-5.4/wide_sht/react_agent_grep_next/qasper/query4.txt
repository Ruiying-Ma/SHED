The paper reports F1 scores rather than a single accuracy metric. Compared to other systems, OpenTapioca scores lower on AIDA-CoNLL (0.482 micro / 0.399 macro vs AIDA 0.725 / 0.684) and RSS-500 (0.335 / 0.310 vs AIDA 0.455 / 0.447), but higher on ISTEX-1000 (0.870 / 0.858 vs the best baseline DBP Spotlight at 0.574 / 0.575) and Microposts 2016 in micro F1 (0.087 vs AIDA 0.056).
