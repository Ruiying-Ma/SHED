The speech alignment quality is evaluated by human annotators using a 3-point audio-text alignment scale: 1 = wrong alignment, 2 = partial alignment with some words or sentences possibly missing, and 3 = correct alignment, allowing non-spoken syllables at the start or end. Two annotators each rated 30 items from each of three bins.
